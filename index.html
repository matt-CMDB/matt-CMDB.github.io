Our Mission

This resource is dedicated to cataloging and exploring the rapidly evolving intersection of Jewish tradition and artificial intelligence. As technology reshapes how we study texts, perform mitzvot, and understand our own humanity, we hope that this platform can serve as a central hub for teachers, scholars, rabbis, technologists, and the curious public. This is also a place for demonstrating what is intended to be a suite of evaluations and recommendations for AI use within the Jewish community.

Help Build This
