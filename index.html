Our Mission

This library is dedicated to cataloging and exploring the rapidly evolving intersection of Jewish tradition and artificial intelligence. As technology reshapes how we study texts, perform mitzvot, and understand our own humanity, we hope that this platform can serve as a central hub for teachers, scholars, rabbis, technologists, and the curious public.
