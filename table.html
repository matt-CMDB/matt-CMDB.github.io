Resource Library

Welcome to the Internet Index of the AI and Judaism Online Resource Library. This interactive index aggregates tools, scholarship, and discussions at the intersection of Jewish tradition and modern artificial intelligence. While such a list cannot possibly include every internet post on AI and Judaism, it is nevertheless only moderately curated. If you have written or know of additional references that help move the conversation forward, please contact us with suggestions or any other feedback.

You can view the library in either Grid mode to scan data quickly, or switch to Cards for more detailed descriptions of each entry. Use the switch at the top-right corner of the page to change viewing modes. You can also use the filter buttons below to narrow your search by Denomination or Audience. ("Grey Literature," by the way, refers to research produced outside of traditional academic publishing channels, but is nevertheless somewhat in-depth or may otherwise resemble academic scholarship.)
